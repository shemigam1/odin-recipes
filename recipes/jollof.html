Jollof Rice Recipe

A plate of delicious jollof rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Chicken
  • Vegetables
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Chilli Pepper

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine tomatoes, scotch bonnet pepper and onions; purée. Pour out half the purée into a bowl;
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat
  3. Stir in the rice until well mixed, then reduce the heat to low.
  4. Cover pot and let cook until rice is al dente, about 45 minutes.
